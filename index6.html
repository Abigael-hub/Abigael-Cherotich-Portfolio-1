Customer Churn Prediction

This model uses selected characteristics, the CLV, and behavioral patterns of all historical customers (including churned customers). Logistic regression and decision tree models were used. From the analysis a number of key insights that were uncovered; (i) no automatic renewal contract had the highest positive relationship with customer churn (ii) 23% of customers with an expired contract renewed and stayed with the company, and (iii) early CLV was not found to be important in predicting customer churn. Key results were used to improve the customer experience.
